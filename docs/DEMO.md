# Hackathon Demo Flow

1. Open the Wave Router dashboard.
2. Confirm the router, provider registry, and demo balance are online.
3. Define a document summarization job with cost, latency, quality, and privacy constraints.
4. Request a route quote.
5. Show why the specialist provider beats the frontier provider for this request.
6. Execute the job.
7. Watch funds reserve, the provider execute, ILP payment simulate, and XRPL settlement prepare.
8. Show the updated balance and returned job receipt.

The current payment and accounting rails are simulated behind production-shaped adapters. The next milestones replace them with TigerBeetle, Open Payments, and XRPL Testnet transactions without changing the routing domain.
